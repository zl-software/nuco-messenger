# App Store listing (English)

**App name:** Nuco

**Subtitle (30 chars max):** Private, sealed messaging

**Promotional text (170 chars max):**
End to end encrypted 1:1 messages that disappear on a timer and live only on your device. No
phone number, no cloud, no tracking.

**Description:**
Nuco is a private 1:1 messenger built around one idea: no one but you and the person you are
talking to should be able to read your messages. Not us, not the server, not anyone who takes
the device.

Every message is sealed on your device with the Signal Protocol (the same modern cryptography
used by the most trusted private messengers) and can only be opened on your device and your
contact's device. The relay that carries your messages is untrusted by design: it only ever
sees unreadable ciphertext, and it never holds a key that could decrypt anything.

Why people choose Nuco:

- End to end encryption on every message, with in person verification so you can confirm you
  are really talking to your contact.
- Disappearing messages: set a timer and messages delete themselves from both devices.
- On device only: your history lives in an encrypted database on your phone. There is no cloud
  backup to leak or subpoena.
- App lock: unlock with Face ID, Touch ID, or a PIN. Locking closes the encrypted database and
  drops the key from memory, so a locked phone reveals nothing.
- Content free notifications: a notification wakes your phone to fetch messages without ever
  sending the text or the sender through Apple.
- Private voice calls: end to end encrypted 1:1 calls, always routed through a relay so the
  person you call never learns your IP address.
- No accounts tied to you: no phone number, no email, no real name required.
- No ads, no analytics, no trackers.

Nuco is deliberately small and focused. Add a contact by scanning their code in person, verify
you match, and start a conversation that stays between the two of you.
