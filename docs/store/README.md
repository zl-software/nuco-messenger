# Store submission assets (drafts)

These are drafts for review before the first App Store submission. They reflect what Nuco
actually does: end to end encrypted 1:1 messaging with an untrusted relay that only ever
forwards ciphertext. Nothing here is wired into the build; the privacy policy must be hosted
at a public URL and pasted into App Store Connect, and the listing text and questionnaire
answers are entered in the console.

Files:
- `privacy-policy.md` : host this at a public URL (for example https://nuco-messenger.com/privacy) and enter that URL in App Store Connect and Google Play.
- `app-store-listing.en.md`, `app-store-listing.de.md` : listing copy for both shipped locales.
- `privacy-nutrition-labels.md` : answers for Apple's App Privacy questionnaire and encryption export compliance.
- `play-data-safety.md` : answers for the Google Play Data Safety form (Android is the later track).

Replace every `REPLACE_WITH_...` placeholder before submitting. The support email and hosted
URLs below are placeholders.
